Frequently Asked Questions

Can I Substitute This Ingredient?

For best results, I suggest using the precise ingredients listed in the recipe whenever possible. I am aware that you might have dietary limitations or simply not have a certain ingredient on hand, but please keep in mind that, without further recipe testing, I am unable to predict how much a substitution will affect the dish's overall flavour or texture. A subpar consequence could also come from using a substitute.

I tried one of your recipes but it did not turn out. What did I do wrong?

Please be aware that since I am not in the kitchen with you, it is very difficult for me to provide a detailed response. Having said that, I only share recipes that have worked well and have been thoroughly tried in my own kitchen, but I am aware that mistakes can be made. It's essential to keep in mind that there are many variables at play here that could lead to a failed outcome rather than blaming the recipe. But with a little more knowledge, we can figure out what went wrong and fix it so that the next time the turnaround is successful.

What is the best way to store leftovers? How long will this keep?

I'm not confident in my ability to respond to these inquiries because I'm not an authority on food safety. The best ways to store food and extend its shelf life should be determined by your own comfort level and sound reasoning.

Can I freeze this?

On dishes that I have personally frozen, I will always offer directions and advice, but regrettably, I do not freeze all of my recipes, so I am not always able to provide an answer. Please exercise caution when freezing and reheating.

Can I use a slow cooker or instant pot for this recipe instead?

Please be aware that without additional recipe testing, it is very difficult to provide precise conversion information. As always, please use your best judgement when adapting my recipes to an Instant Pot or slow cooker.

Can you share the nutritional information for this recipe?

Sadly, since I am not a certified dietician, I don't feel comfortable revealing the nutritional facts for any of my recipes. To obtain such information, I advise you to use online calorie counters at your discretion.
