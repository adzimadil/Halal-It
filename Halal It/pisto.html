Pisto

BY MATT
July 2, 2022

Pisto is a Spanish dish originally from the Region of Murcia, Castilla La Mancha and Extremadura. It is made of tomatoes, onions, eggplant or courgettes, green and red peppers, and olive oil

5.0 (20+)

  • YIELDS

    2-3 servings

  • PREP TIME

    44 minutes

  • BEST SERVE WITH

    toasted bread or bagguette

  • OUR VERSION

    we Halal It by excluding red wine

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 pound kimchi, cut into bite size pieces
  • ¼ cup kimchi brine
  • ½ package of tofu, sliced into ½ inch thick bite size pieces
  • 3 scallion
  • 1 medium onion, sliced (1 cup)
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons gochugaru (Korean hot pepper flakes)
  • 1 tablespoon gochujang (hot pepper paste)
  • teaspoon toasted sesame oil
  • 2 cups of anchovy stock (can alternate with chicken or beef stock)

DIRECTIONS

step 1

Place the kimchi and kimchi brine in a shallow pot. Add onion

step 2

Slice 2 scallion diagonally and add them to the pot

step 3

Add salt, sugar, hot pepper flakes, and hot pepper paste. Drizzle sesame oil over top and add the anchovy stock

step 4

Cover and cook for 10 minutes over medium high heat

step 5

Open and mix in the seasonings with a spoon. Lay the tofu over top.

step 6

Cover and cook another 10 to 15 minutes over medium heat

step 7

Chop 1 scallion and put it on the top of the stew. Remove from the heat and serve right away with rice.
